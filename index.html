GenZ(ine)

by Munus Shih

This zine is about...

p5.(gen)Zine is free and friendly p5 community library created by Munus Shih and Iley Cao for anyone curious about creative code and zine-making. You are free to experiment with the idea of collaborative zine-coding, forking, remixing and explore what generative zine can do to contribute to community building.
